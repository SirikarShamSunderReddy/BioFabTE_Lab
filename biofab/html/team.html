Principal Investigator

Dr. Falguni Pati

Professor & Head, Department of Biomedical Engineering, IIT Hyderabad

Dr. Falguni Pati is Professor and Head of the Department of Biomedical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Hyderabad. He received his Ph.D. from IIT Kharagpur and gained international research experience through postdoctoral appointments at POSTECH, South Korea, and KTH Royal Institute of Technology, Sweden, before joining IITH in 2015.

He leads the Biofabrication and Tissue Engineering (BioFabTE) Laboratory, where his research focuses on advancing tissue engineering through 3D bioprinting, with particular emphasis on bioink development, bioprinting strategies, and the fabrication of functional tissue and organ models for in vitro and translational applications.

His contributions span research, education, and translation, including introducing India's first dedicated Biofabrication course at IITH and delivering the NPTEL course "Organ Printing".
